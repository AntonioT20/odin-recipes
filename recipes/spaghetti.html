Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is probably the most delicious pasta recipe to ever exist! Serve this easy Spaghetti with your choice of breadsticks and a healthy amount of paramsian.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef on medium heat untill there is no more pink.
  2. Wash the beef with cold water.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce to a sauce pan with the beef and simmer on a medium-low heat for 10mins.
  4. Add a splash of olive oil and a pinch of salt to a pot of water and bring to a rolling boil.
  5. Add noodles to boiling water and let boil for 10mins.
  6. Drain noodles and add another splash of olive oil to keep noodles from sticking.
  7. Serve noodles with spaghetti sauce on top, grated parmesian, and optionally garlic bread.
